We are Personae Labs, an R&D lab investigating the future of human expression online. We believe that the availability of advanced cryptography in consumer devices and the rise of public social networks will completely alter the shape of digital society. Read our full manifesto.

If you’re excited by zero knowledge proofs, digital identity where you control your own data, or personable social design, please reach out by twitter or email: we are especially excited to hear from traditionally underrepresented groups to ensure that everyone is included in the future of identity.
